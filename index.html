Swarm Intelligence
for Disasters

A drone-based ecosystem designed to provide rapid aerial mapping and survival kit deployment in disaster zones using autonomous detection and swarm protocols.

Project Overview

The project aims to develop a deployable swarm of lightweight, low-cost UAVs utilizing decentralized communication and onboard AI to detect victims autonomously in disaster-stricken areas. Rapid response is critical when infrastructure is compromised, making ground access impossible.

Problem Statement

No open-source, micro-class (<70g) UAV platform exists that is cost-effective (<PKR 15k) while supporting decentralized communication and Edge AI.

Key Objective

Autonomous hazard assessment (Fire, Smoke, Humans) processed locally on the drone to minimize bandwidth usage in disaster zones.
