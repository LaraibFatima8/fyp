Project Overview

The project aims to develop a deployable swarm of lightweight, low-cost UAVs utilizing decentralized communication and onboard AI to detect victims autonomously in disaster-stricken areas. Rapid response is critical when infrastructure is compromised, making ground access impossible.

Problem Statement

Scaling UAV swarms and deploying onboard Edge AI usually requires high-end, heavy, and cost-prohibitive hardware, making experimental validation difficult within strict budget constraints.

Key Objective

A lightweight, low-cost micro-UAV proof-of-concept designed specifically to bench-test swarm networking protocols and localized AI processing without the need for expensive, deployment-grade infrastructure.
